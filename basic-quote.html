Brief Overview of Our Standard Plan

Our Standard Plan delivers essential residential interior design services, encompassing comprehensive design consultations, personalized floor plans, and expert project management. We pride ourselves on utilizing high-quality materials and maximizing space efficiency, offering exceptional value as we transform your home into a stylish and comfortable sanctuary.

We offer a comprehensive range of interior design services tailored to fit your budget, ensuring meticulous planning and execution of every project detail:

  • Detailed Floor Plans: Precise layouts optimizing space and functionality.
  • Elevation Drawings: Visual representations providing a clear view of the final design.
  • 3D Renderings: Realistic visualizations bringing your vision to life.
  • Custom Design Solutions: Tailored approaches meeting your specific needs and preferences.
  • Cost-Effective Planning: High-quality services within your budgetary constraints.

Stage 1:

  • Initial site visit for accurate measurements
  • Concept layout including furniture placement and Vastu incorporation
  • Concept presentation with reference images for design finalization
  • Plumbing layout

Stage 2:

  • 3D views of living room, toilets, and kitchen (as applicable)
  • Detailed 2D drawings for toilets and kitchens, including electrical layout, flooring, false ceiling, and services
  • 2D drawings for tile placement
  • False ceiling and flooring details for living areas
  • Vendor management system implementation

Stage 3:

  • 3D views of bedrooms, children's rooms, and study areas
  • Comprehensive false ceiling, flooring, and electrical details

Stage 4:

  • Wall paneling details for all rooms
  • Loose and fixed furniture specifications
  • Project management and timeline monitoring
  • Remote consultation via audio/video calls

Stage 5:

  • Kitchen cabinetry details
  • Final site visit (if required)
  • Color scheme finalization
  • On-site material sample selection
  • Compilation of all design drawings
  • GST invoice issuance
