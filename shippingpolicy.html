Shipping Policy

Welcome to INTER'OR By ArchitectsHive's Shipping Policy

We're dedicated to providing you with clear and transparent information about how we deliver our products to you. Below, you'll find details on the delivery process, shipping charges, international shipping, and our policy on refunds and returns.

Delivery of House Plans

At INTER'OR By ArchitectsHive, we understand the importance of timely access to house plans for your projects. That's why we offer digital delivery of house plans in PDF format. Once you've successfully completed your order and payment, you'll receive an email containing a secure link to download your purchased house plans. This digital delivery method ensures quick and convenient access to your architectural designs.

Delivery Time

Your satisfaction is our priority, and we aim to deliver your digital house plans promptly. Upon confirmation of your order and receipt of payment, you can expect to receive your house plans within 1-2 business days. Please note that delivery times may vary depending on factors such as order volume and technical processing. Rest assured, we strive to minimize any delays and provide you with timely access to your architectural designs.

Shipping Charges

INTER'OR By ArchitectsHive is committed to providing affordable and accessible architectural solutions. As part of our commitment to customer satisfaction, we do not impose any shipping charges on the delivery of our digital house plans. You'll only be charged for the cost of the house plans as displayed on our website. With no additional shipping fees, you can enjoy cost-effective access to high-quality architectural designs.

International Shipping

Whether you're located locally or internationally, INTER'OR By ArchitectsHive makes it easy for you to access our house plans. Our digital products can be purchased and downloaded from anywhere in the world. We do not impose any geographical restrictions on the delivery of our digital house plans. Wherever you are, you can rely on INTER'OR By ArchitectsHive to provide you with architectural solutions tailored to your needs.

Refunds and Returns

At INTER'OR By ArchitectsHive, we're committed to your satisfaction with our products and services. However, due to the nature of digital products and the instant access provided upon purchase, we do not offer refunds or returns for house plans. We encourage customers to review the product details and specifications carefully before making a purchase decision. If you have any questions or concerns about our house plans, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our customer support team is here to assist you and ensure that you're fully satisfied with your purchase.

Return Policy

Overview

INTER'OR By ArchitectsHive is committed to ensuring customer satisfaction with every purchase. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Refunds

Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item.

If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. You will receive the refund within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for the same or different house plan, please contact us to discuss the options available.

Contact Us

If you have any further questions or inquiries regarding our shipping policy or any other aspect of our services, please feel free to contact us at contact@architectshive.com. Our dedicated customer support team is available to address any concerns you may have and provide you with the assistance you need.
